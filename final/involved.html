Get Involved

With your help we can save the flying foxes. Since most of us can't travel all the way to the island of Mauritius, here are some ways you can help from your own home.

There are two ways you can help; donating time, and donating money. By donating your time you can email polititians and share the word to others. By donating money, your funds will go directly to helping the fruit bats by funding sanctuaries and securing local support.
